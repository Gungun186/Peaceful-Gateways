Tour guide to visit Uttrakhand



        
OCT-MAR

            What To Expect: Cool to cold weather with temperatures ranging from 3°C to 15°C
                            (37°F to 59°F) in most regions. Higher altitudes, like Auli and 
                            Nainital, can experience snowfall, making it a popular time for 
                            winter sports and scenic beauty.
            Things You'll Love: experiencing the serene beauty of the snow-capped Himalayas, engaging 
                                in winter sports like skiing in Auli, and exploring the charming hill
                                stations like Nainital and Mussoorie. The cooler temperatures make it 
                                a perfect time for trekking and enjoying the scenic landscapes. 
                
        
APR-JUN

            What To Expect: warm to hot weather with temperatures ranging from 20°C to 35°C.
            Things You'll Love: Exploring the hill stations like Nainital and Mussoorie to escape the 
                                heat, enjoying the scenic beauty of the Himalayas, and experiencing the 
                                vibrant local festivals. 

            
JUL-SEP

            What To Expect: Monsoon season with moderate to heavy rainfall and high humidity. 
                            Daytime temperatures typically range from 20°C to 30°C.
            Things You'll Love: Experiencing the lush greenery and rejuvenated landscapes brought by 
                                the monsoon rains. cooler temperatures make it a pleasant time to explore 
                                the scenic beauty and adventure activities.
