Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Travel Questions

1. How do I book a trip?
You can book a trip through our website by selecting your desired destination, choosing your travel dates, and completing the booking form. You can also contact our customer support team for assistance.
2. What is included in my travel package?
Our travel packages typically include flights, accommodation, transportation, and sightseeing tours. Specific inclusions will vary depending on the package you choose.
3. Can I customize my itinerary?
Yes! We offer customizable travel packages. You can select your preferred destinations, activities, and accommodations. Contact our team for further details and to create your perfect itinerary.
4. Are meals included in the travel package?
The inclusion of meals depends on the specific package you choose. Some packages include all meals, while others offer breakfast only or have meals at additional cost.
5. Do I need travel insurance?
While travel insurance is not mandatory, it is highly recommended to protect yourself against unforeseen circumstances like trip cancellations, lost baggage, or medical emergencies.

Destination-Specific Questions

6. What are the best times to visit India?
The best time to visit India depends on the region. Generally, October to March is the most favorable time as the weather is pleasant, especially in the north. For the beaches of Goa and Kerala, the winter months are ideal.
7. What are the top places to visit in Switzerland?
Switzerland is renowned for its picturesque landscapes. Top destinations include the Swiss Alps (for skiing and hiking), Lucerne, Zermatt, Interlaken, and the cities of Zurich and Geneva.
8. Is Dubai a family-friendly destination?
Yes, Dubai is a fantastic destination for families. It has numerous attractions such as Dubai Parks and Resorts, the Dubai Aquarium, the Global Village, and various shopping malls with entertainment options.
