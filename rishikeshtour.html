Best time to visit Rishikesh



        
OCT-MAR

            What To Expect: Rishikesh enjoys cool and pleasant weather, with temperatures 
                    ranging from 6°C to 20°C (43°F to 68°F). It's an ideal time for outdoor 
                    activities like river rafting, hiking, and exploring the natural beauty of the area.
            Things You'll Love : Rishikesh offers tranquil weather and stunning views. You'll 
                    love yoga by the Ganges, hiking in the Himalayas, river rafting, and exploring 
                    spiritual ashrams. 🧘‍♂️🏞️🚣

        
APR-JUN

            What To Expect: Rishikesh experiences hot and humid weather, with temperatures
                    ranging from 30°C to 38°C (86°F to 100°F). It's the start of the hot season,
                    with increasing humidity and occasional rain showers. Be prepared for the heat 
                    and stay hydrated!
            Things You'll Love: Rishikesh offers vibrant festivals, river rafting adventures, 
                    spiritual retreats, and serene yoga sessions by the Ganges. Enjoy the natural 
                    beauty and cultural richness of this spiritual hub! 🌿🧘‍♂️🚣‍♂️

            
JUL-SEP

            What To Expect: Rishikesh experiences hot and rainy weather, with temperatures ranging 
                    from 25°C to 33°C (77°F to 91°F). Expect frequent afternoon showers and high humidity.
                    It's a good idea to pack light, breathable clothing and an umbrella!
            Things You'll Love: Rishikesh offers lush greenery after monsoon rains, thrilling river
                    rafting, serene yoga sessions, and vibrant cultural festivals. Enjoy the rejuvenating
                    atmosphere and scenic beauty of this spiritual hub! 🌿🚣‍♂️🧘‍♂️
