Best time to visit Delhi



        
OCT-MAR

            What To Expect: Foggy mornings and cold evenings. 
                            Days are warm and pleasant when sun rises, and 
                            chilly when it doesn't.
            Things You'll Love :Open-air cultural events, picnics, 
                                shopping, rooftop brunches and city sightseeing.

        
APR-JUN

            What To Expect:Average rainfall with mild humidity.
            Things You'll Love:Nature walks, monument visits, 
                               mall/pub hopping.

            
JUL-SEP

            What To Expect:Hot and sunny days.
            Things You'll Love:Going to spas and night clubs 
                               and attending seasonal fruit 
                               (especially mango) festivals.
