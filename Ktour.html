Best time to visit Karnatka



        
OCT-MAR

            What To Expect: Pleasant and mild weather with daytime temperatures 
                            ranging from 15°C to 30°C. Coastal regions may be slightly 
                            warmer, around 20°C to 35°C. It's an ideal time to explore 
                            the state's natural beauty and cultural heritage.
            Things You'll Love : Exploring the historical marvels of Hampi, savoring the 
                                 aromatic coffee in Coorg, and basking in the serene 
                                 beaches of Gokarna.

        
APR-JUN

            What To Expect: Hot and humid weather with temperatures ranging from 32°C to 38°C. 
                            The onset of the monsoon in June brings heavy rains, providing some 
                            relief from the heat.
            Things You'll Love: Vibrant atmosphere at the Chinnaswamy Stadium during IPL 
                                cricket matches,the lush green landscapes after pre-monsoon
                                 showers, and the refreshing local mangoes.

            
JUL-SEP

            What To Expect: Monsoon season with heavy rainfall, high humidity, and temperatures 
                            ranging from 25°C to 32°C.
            Things You'll Love: Lush greenery brought by the monsoon rains, the serene beauty of 
                                waterfalls like Jog Falls at their peak, and the vibrant atmosphere 
                                during Ganesh Chaturthi celebrations.
