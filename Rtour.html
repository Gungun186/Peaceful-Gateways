Best time to visit Rajasthan



        
OCT-MAR

            What To Expect: Dry and cool weather with daytime temperatures 
                            ranging from 25°C to 35°C and cooler nights around 
                            10°C to 20°C. It's an ideal time to explore the 
                            state's magnificent palaces, forts, and vibrant festivals 
                            without the extreme heat.
            Things You'll Love: Exploring majestic forts and palaces, experiencing the 
                                vibrant culture and traditions during festivals like Diwali 
                                and the Pushkar Camel Fair, and enjoying cool desert nights 
                                under a starry sky.

        
APR-JUN

            What To Expect: Hot and dry weather with temperatures often soaring 
                            above 40°C (104°F) during the day
            Things You'll Love: Exploring the majestic forts and palaces in the early mornings 
                                or late evenings to beat the heat, experiencing the vibrant 
                                desert life, and indulging in delicious Rajasthani thalis.

            
JUL-SEP

            What To Expect: Monsoon season with moderate rainfall and high humidity.
            Things You'll Love: Witnessing the vibrant landscapes as the desert blooms after the rains, 
                                exploring the majestic forts and palaces with fewer crowds, and enjoying 
                                the cultural festivals and fairs that come alive during the monsoon.
