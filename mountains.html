Kashmir

Sikkim

Kashmir, known as "Paradise on Earth," is famous for its stunning landscapes, such as Dal Lake with floating gardens and houseboats, the vibrant Tulip Garden, and saffron fields. It boasts rich handicrafts like Pashmina shawls and Kashmiri carpets, unique cuisine featuring Wazwan, and cultural landmarks like the Amarnat Cave.🌟🏰💞

Sikkim, nestled in the Himalayas, is India's first fully organic state. It's home to Kanchenjunga, the third-highest peak in the world, and boasts stunning landscapes, rich biodiversity, and a harmonious blend of culture ☺️💫🏔

North_East

Utttarakhand

Northeast India is a biodiversity hotspot, home to rare species like the red panda and one-horned rhino, along with vibrant cultures, unique cuisines, and the living root bridges of Meghalaya🤗🌙🌉

Uttarakhand, known as the "Land of Gods," is home to sacred sites like Kedarnath and Badrinath and the source of the Ganga and Yamuna rivers, nestled in the majestic Himalayas ,it is a hub for adventure sports, including trekking, river rafting in Rishikesh, and skiing in Auli😇👏🕉
